The Bachelorette, Season 19

Show History

The Bachelorette is a romantic reality TV show that has aired on ABC for the past 19 years. In it, the female lead (or bachelorette) meets 30+ single men that ultimately compete for her affections. On the last few episodes of the series, the lead chooses the man she wants to marry, and he must propose to her to show his commitment to a long-term future with her. Hosted by Chris Harrison, every season of The Bachelorette promises more dramatic scenes than the last.

This Season

This season marked two unpredecented moments in the show's history: Firstly, it gave us the the oldest bachelorette to be cast - Clare Crawley - a reasonable 38 years old. Secondly, it gave us the first bachelorette to be quickly replaced by another lead, because the first lead went all in on a male cast member very early on in the season.

Tayshia Adams

The show's current lead is Tayshia Adams.
